#My E-commerce Business

#Business Name
BookStore

##Business Description
BookStore is an online bookstore where customers can browse, search, and puchase books throung a website.

##Target Customers
-Students
-University students
-Working people
-General readers

##Products
-Novels
-Comics
-Educational books
-Children's books

##Problems
-Customers may not have time to visit a bookstore.
-it is difficult to compare books from different stores.
-Some books are unavailable in local bookstores.

##Solution
-Customers can search for books online.
-Customers can order books anytime.
-Customers can pay online.
-Customers can track their orders.

##Features
-Register and login
-Search books
-View book details
-Add to cart
-Place an order
-Online payment
-Order tracking
